Account Deletion Request

mkideabox, Co. Ltd (엠케이아이디어박스 주식회사) provides a simple way to request deletion of your account and associated data.

Steps to Request Deletion

- Please contact us at support@mkideabox.com with the subject "Account Deletion Request".
- We will process your request and confirm once completed.

Data Deleted

- Google account link used for login
- Any personal information associated with your account

Data Retained

- This app does not store user data on its own servers.
- Advertising data collected by Google AdMob is managed by Google and not by us.
- We do not retain any of your account data after deletion.

For any questions, please contact: mkideabox@gmail.com
